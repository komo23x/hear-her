bio / soleilroz

soleilroz is a vinyl music curator and creative based in california. her sound and selections are shaped by identity and curiosity, guided by feeling rather than genre. working exclusively with vinyl, she approaches djing as a ritual, slowing down, listening deeply, and honoring the physical act of selecting and playing records.
